Weapons:
-Warp Bow:
---Warp Bow can now fire Stone Arrows for a cheap but low damage option.
---Unequipping the Unique/Legendary Warp Bow removes the tether from any dinos that are currently tethered by it.
---Quantum Entanglement bonus "All Arrows Deal Raid Torpor" replaced with "Can Fire Metal Arrows". This also works for both regular metal and knockout arrows.
---String Theory bonus "Arrows Deal Metal Arrow Damages" replaced with "300% Arrow Damage."
---String Theory Bonus "Cosmic Grenades For Ammo Create Starfires" replaced with "10% Chance To Create Starfire On Boss Hit"

Quantum:
-Species/Variant paragons AND Variant souls can now be stored in personal/tribe storage. All of them currently in soul compressors will be automatically sent to tribe storage (or personal, if not in a tribe).
-Paragon compressing functionality in the soul compressor removed. Void Vacuum will automatically compress to tribe storage (or personal, if not in a tribe).
-Stat potions have their own UI to pull them as needed. When pulling all-stats/all-types, if enough points exist, you will pull unique and legendary versions.
-Boss Souls, Master Souls, Group Master Souls, Unique Group Master Souls, and Aspect Souls are now all compressible in the Quantum Storage system.
-Merging tribes will now merge their quantum storages. 

Variant Reworks:
-Nuclear:
---Nuclear is now an "activated" buff, meaning that you press the button once to activate and the explosion will happen after the full build-up. This will remove the conflict it has with saddle added variants. 
---Full buildup time is now 5 seconds instead of 10. It will always deal 20x ability damage with the explosion, at maximum radius.
---This requires 50% stamina to activate and will drain 100% of the current stamina after the explosion triggers, as well as a debuff the Nuclear with a short stun.

-Lethal:
---Stacks now multiply damage taken by 1.03 instead of by 3 (Exponentially: 1.03^Stacks, capping at roughly 19.2x damage multiplier).
---Stacks now cap at 100 instead of 5.
---The debuff no longer reduces the enemies melee damage by 30%.
---The debuff now lasts for 10 seconds instead of 300 but is refreshed when a new stack is applied. All stacks are lost if the buff times out. 
---Lethal can now only be applied through melee damage and not ability damage.
---Lethal is now always applied when dealing melee damage instead of by % chance.
