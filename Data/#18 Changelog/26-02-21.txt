Ark Omega 2/21 Patch Notes:

Utility Variant Reworks:
Crafting:
---Immune to Damage Impulse.
---Gains 95% damage reduction when over-encumbered.
Farming: 
---Can water crops without need for an unstable water.
---In combat, will consume 1 fertilizer when hit to reduce damage taken by 50%.
---Will no longer randomly drop 1 fertilizer when killed.

Other Variant Reworks:
Armored:
-Damage reduction % is now exponential and grows at you lose health instead of equal to % of missing health for a non-linear DR.
-Armored DR now maxes at 96% instead of 98%, and still loses all DR entirely below 1% health as before.
-Armored can no longer take more than 10% of its max health as damage in a single hit.
Gorgon:
-Gorgon stone is no longer dealt through damage and cannot be dodged. Ghosts stoned by Gorgon can also not dodge.
Ghost:
-Ghosts stoned by Gorgon can no longer dodge.
-Ghost bosses no longer always have 50% dodge and now follow the same tier-based dodge chance as everything else.

Others:
-Fixed a bug with Omega NPCs spawning with base stats.
-Corrupt variants on Extinction replaced with normal Omega spawns.
-Gave a proper ability damage value to the Ceratosaurus.
-Omega glider no longer has durability.
-Fixed a bug causing some flyers to struggle to land due to Wildcard's addition of Sparse Data causing runtime edits to apply to every dino of that species regardless of circumstance.
-Fixed mate boost and attack range bugs related to the above Wildcard shenanigans.
-Fixed a bug causing dinos sent to the Reliquary to lose their saddles.
-Fixed a bug where Nuclear was being treated as Explosive for damage and radius.
-Fixed a bug preventing the DodoRex from taking torpor properly.
