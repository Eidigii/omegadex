Crafting Requirements:
Metal Ingot: 20
Polymer: 10
Ethereal Soul: 3
Basic Essence: 15

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Dimensional Dumpster is crafted in the omega workbench. It is a structure you can assign
items you wish to be destroyed without trace to, and have them sent directly there when
dropped into a void vacuum.
