Crafting Requirements:
Metal Ingot: 20
Mythical Soul: 3
Basic Essence: 15

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Scroll Storage is crafted in the omega workbench. It is a customizable structure that can
hold blueprints and quest scrolls, and have them sorted to it. Automatic sorting options
include by quality (primitive-ascendant), by type (weapon, saddle, armor, quest scroll), and an
auto-destroy function.
